With Associates

All good things

When we started With Associates in 2004 it was still tricky for many businesses to get online and manage their digital presence. We set up with the objective to help them to do that.

Since then we have seen the conception and growth of Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, Shopify, Campaign Monitor and Squarespace – to name just a few of the free, social and cloud-hosted services on offer – while the invention of smart phones has spawned an ecosystem that was utterly unimaginable when we began.

Now, after 12 years of service, growth and evolution within this ever changing digital sector, we have decided that our original objective has been met, and that the most helpful and exciting challenges lie ahead, on new and different paths. To this end, we have decided that it’s time to close the chapter on With Associates.

While in many ways it is a sad decision to make, we are at the same time immensely proud of the work we have done, and even more personally, of the teams that we’ve been lucky enough to build and work alongside.

Thank you, to each and every associate who has helped us make it possible.

With Love,
Erin, Jenifer and Mathew,
